I’m an award winning game designer with 5 years experience designing games for all ages. I’m currently lead designer on 15 new games for Skillsville, a new PBS property.

I’m interested in creating deceptively simple, innovative games that integrate theme seamlessly. I love working with narrative, especially within established IPs, and can fit a puzzle into almost anything I make.

I’m especially proud of my work in Décorum, which has gotten great reviews and will be translated into several new languages this year, as well as my work on Hero Elementary, which received millions of plays and several awards.

I’m always open to new opportunities. If you’d like to contact me, you can email me at charliejmackin@gmail.com or reach out on Twitter or LinkedIn.
